Tuesday Social Ride

Every Tuesday at 7pm (April – September) we have a social ride where the pace is kept to a reasonable 12 – 15 mph and noone is left behind

Tuesday Training Ride

Every Tuesday at 7pm (April – September) we have a training ride for those who want to push themeselves that little bit harder Pace 15-18mph

Thursday Beginners

Every Thursday at 7pm (April – September) we have a beginners ride where the pace is slow and the focus is on gaining confidence

Every Sunday at 9am (April – September) we have a longer ride where the pace is kept slower. Coffee and Cake stops are also involved
